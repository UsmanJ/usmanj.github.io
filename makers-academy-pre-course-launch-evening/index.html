Makers Academy Pre-Course Launch Evening

August 3rd was the day I first went in to meet my fellow students at Makers. As I got to the building straight away I met a new student like myself. By the time I was upstairs I had met 3 new people who were ambitious and pumped up ready to learn.

The launch was short but sweet. All the students of the September cohort introduced themselves. Students were from a wide variety of backgrounds; lawyers to a student fresh out of high school. Nikesh continued the evening by introducing us to GitHub, Slack and the Pre-course material. At the end of the short launch; some students went off to the local bar down the road and others headed home.

The excitement has been building for my Makers journey and I hope to share all my experiences with you guys. I hope to learn a lot from this experience and am very determined to make the most of it. Follow my blog to get regular updates on my progress and my makers experience.

Good luck to all my peers.

Usman
