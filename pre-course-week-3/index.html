Pre-Course Week 3

It's been a crazy week! Chris Pine tried to take over my life. But I dumped him for Ruby Kickstart.

As we were told at the pre-course launch, after week 2 things were going to get real hard. And they were. Ruby literally took over my life. The torture of being so close to solving a challenge yet being so far. My head started to hurt by the end of the week. But theres no giving up! In fact it feels like a game, its so addictive. You don't even feel like your working/studying. It's just so fun. My peers are all so much fun too. The environment is so cool! I'm loving it!

Anyway so I managed to complete Ruby Kickstarter (going to try and complete Chris Pine this week) and I've managed to get 70 points on codewars. I'm going to try and get 125 by the end of the week (fingers crossed).

I've had a long week so I'm going to hit the bed. Need to be up early tomorrow for some more Ruby! See what I mean, its addictive!

Adios for now.

Usman
